Phoebe Nichole B. Lozada

Hi, my name is Phoebe Nichole Baroy Lozada and I'm a nail artist. I started doing nail extensions as a way to express my creativity and to honor my grandfather, Eugenio, who passed away. I have always had a passion for beauty and fashion, and I saw nail extensions as a way to bring together my creative skills and my love for making others feel confident and beautiful.


The strength that inspires me to do this is my grandfather, whose name was Eugenio. He was a loving and caring person who always encouraged me to follow my dreams and to never give up on what I love. To pay tribute to him, I named my nail business "Nails by E", which stands for "Nails by Eugenio". This name is a constant reminder of my grandfather's love and support and it keeps me motivated to keep creating beautiful and unique nail designs.


I take pride in my work and I always strive to create the best possible experience for my clients. Whether it's creating a unique design for a special occasion or simply enhancing someone's natural beauty, I am dedicated to making sure each of my clients leaves feeling confident and beautiful.


Thank you for taking the time to read about me and my passion for nail extensions. I look forward to helping you bring your vision to life.
